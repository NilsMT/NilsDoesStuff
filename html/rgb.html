How it's calculated ?

Well first our eyes are most sensitive to green, then red and blue.
So, by some wizardry we obtain ratios. Then we take the R,G and B of our color and make this calculation :

0.3 x R + 0.59 x G + 0.11 x B

This allows us to get a number, that will be the same for R,G and B of our grayscaled color !
