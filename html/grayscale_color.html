What's a grayscaled color ?

Its the color that is the same in grayscale.
It allow you to know how your color will look if the saturation is set to 0,
when you print something only in black and white for example.

How it's calculated ?

Our eyes are most sensitive to green, then red and blue.
So, we obtain ratios according to that sensitivity (0.3 for red, 0.59 for green and 0.11 for blue).
Then we take the R,G and B of our color and make this calculation :

0.3 x R + 0.59 x G + 0.11 x B

This allows us to get a number, that will be the same for R,G and B of our grayscaled color !
